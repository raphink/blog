A young man got into a store.  
He asked to the angel that was here, behind the cass : \`\`What do you sell here, my good angel?''  
The angel answered him with a large smile \`\`We have everything you want''.  
\`\`Then I would like the wars to stop, a better life for marginal people, work for unemployed guys, the end of...'' The angels stopped him \`\`I'm sorry, young man, but it seems you did not read the panel on the door. Here we don't sell fruits, only seeds. Did you think about buying seeds of peace, of generosity, of joy, of courage? Also don't be worried, as the store will still be opened after Christmas''.
