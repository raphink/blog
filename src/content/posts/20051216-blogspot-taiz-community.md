Taizé is an ecumenical Christian community in Bourgogne, France. It has been founded by brother Roger, and gathers now more than a hundred brothers. Taizé welcomes every year thousands of young people from all over the world and the community organizes meetings of tens of thousands people in European great cities.  
  
You can find out much more about Taizé on [their official site](http://web.archive.org/web/20050308232548/http://www.taize.fr/).  
  
You can also check the [Taizé Community](http://web.archive.org/web/20050308232548/http://taize.multiply.com/) on Multiply.
