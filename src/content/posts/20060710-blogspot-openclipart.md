I was searching for Christian open-source clipart today. Unfortunately I didn’t find any. All the free Christian clipart I found have restrictive - or even **very** restrictive - licenses ![:(](http://web.archive.org/web/20060805074200/http://raphink.info/blog/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_sad.gif)

However, I have found one open-source clipart website - although not Christian - with very good things on it [http://www.openclipart.org](http://web.archive.org/web/20060805074200/http://www.openclipart.org/).

I strongly encourage all graphic developers to contribute to this project ![:)](http://web.archive.org/web/20060805074200/http://raphink.info/blog/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif)
