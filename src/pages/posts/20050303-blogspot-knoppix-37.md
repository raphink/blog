Well I've been using knoppix for a long time now, but I thought I would write a review on it though.  
  
You have heard of linux and wonder about what it is? You would like to try it but don't want to take any risk with your windows box?  
  
Then you need to use a live CD, and knoppix is the best you can try imho :)  
  
All you have to do is download a CD image of Knoppix and burn it to a CD, and then reboot your computer on the CD.  
  
You can find sources to download the CD image of Knoppix on [the knoppix site](http://web.archive.org/web/20050308232548/http://www.knoppix.net/get.php).  
  
Enjoy!
